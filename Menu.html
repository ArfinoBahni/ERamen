Our Ramen

Tonkotsu Ramen

Rich and creamy pork bone broth enhanced with garlic oil served with fresh thin noodles made with organic flour, braised pork belly, creamy soft-boiled egg, and green onions.

Shoyu Ramen

Light soy sauce based broth with high umami flavour, served with firmly cooked noodles, braised pork belly, creamy soft-boiled egg, green onions, wakame, and mushrooms. (Available to be served with chicken)

Miso Ramen

Broth made with fermented soybean paste with deep and complex flavour profile, served with fresh noodles, braised pork belly, green onions, nori seaweed, soft-boiled egg, and corn. (Available to be served with chicken)

Matcha Ramen

Our innovative twist on the classic japanese dish, with vibrant and earthy flavours of our green tea broth infusing every element in the dish, made with a mix of chicken and vegetable stock, mixed with high-quality matcha powder. Served with fresh noodles, crispy chicken katsu, naruto, and green onions.

Tsukemen

Also known as dipping ramen, this dish offers a unique way of eating the beloved japanese dish. Our dipping broth is made with a combination of bonito flakes, soy sauce, and pork stock. Accompanied by our fresh thick and chewy noodles that holds on to the sauce for a satisfying bite. Served with soft-boiled egg, braised pork belly, naruto, and green onions.
